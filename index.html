About Dineo

Multan's Go-To Spot for
Good Food & Good Vibes

Dineo Fast Food & Cafe Lounge started with one simple idea — great food shouldn't be complicated. We wanted a place in Multan where you could walk in hungry and leave genuinely satisfied, whether it's a solo lunch, a family dinner, or a late-night hangout with friends.

We're located inside Emirates Mall, Model Town — easy to find, easy to get to, and open almost every hour that matters. From crispy burgers to creamy shakes, desi handi to cafe-style pasta, our menu is built to cover all cravings without making you think too hard.

Everything is made fresh. We don't cut corners on ingredients, and our kitchen takes real pride in what comes out of it. That's why our customers keep coming back — and why Multan knows the name Dineo.
